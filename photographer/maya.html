Maya Rodriguez

📍 New York City, New York, USA

Maya Rodriguez is a celebrated portrait and editorial photographer whose studio sits in the heart of Brooklyn, New York. With an instinct for finding the soul behind every face, Maya creates images that are as emotionally resonant as they are technically flawless. Her editorial work has appeared in Vogue, Harper's Bazaar, and The New York Times Magazine. Maya has collaborated with top talent agencies, A-list celebrities, and luxury fashion brands, and her fine-art portraits have been shown in galleries from SoHo to Paris.
