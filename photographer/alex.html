Alex Chen

📍 Denver, Colorado, USA

Alex Chen is a celebrated landscape and nature photographer based in Denver, Colorado. With over a decade behind the lens, Alex has traversed thousands of miles of wilderness — from the jagged peaks of the Rocky Mountains to the vast silence of the Mojave Desert. His golden-hour compositions and dramatic long exposures reveal nature in ways most people never get to witness. Alex's work has been published in National Geographic, Outdoor Photographer, and featured in galleries across North America.
