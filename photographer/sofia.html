Sofia Andersson

📍 Tokyo, Japan

Sofia Andersson is a Swedish-born street and documentary photographer who traded Stockholm's winters for Tokyo's electric chaos. Armed with nothing but a 35mm lens and an instinct for the decisive moment, Sofia weaves through the neon-lit alleys of Shinjuku and Shibuya, capturing unscripted stories of urban humanity. Her raw, unfiltered imagery has been recognised by the World Press Photo foundation, featured in TIME magazine, and exhibited across Tokyo, Berlin, and Stockholm. Sofia believes every city has a heartbeat — and she's dedicated to recording it one frame at a time.
